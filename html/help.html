Welcome to TechThrift!

Your Guide to Shopping Sustainably:


How to Buy

  • Search for products: Use the search bar at the top to find used electronics.
  • Browse categories: Use the navigation bar to explore products by type (e.g., laptops, phones).
  • Add to cart: Click to add items to your shopping cart.
  • Checkout: Visit your cart and proceed to checkout. You’ll be asked for shipping and payment info.

Using Wishlist

Click the icon to save items you like for later viewing in your Wishlist.
You can also set and view product alerts in your wishlist.

Order Tracking

Go to your profile by clicking the icon, and view your recent orders and shipping status.

Returns & Refunds

  • You can return most items within 14 days of delivery.
  • Go to your orders, select the item, and click “Return Item”.
  • Once received, we’ll issue a refund to your original payment method.

Need More Help?

Secure Shopping

TechThrift uses encrypted payments and verified sellers to ensure your safety and satisfaction.
